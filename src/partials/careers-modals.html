Apply your cv to lead unity developer

Lead unity developer

Our company is actively evolving, and now we are looking for a Lead Unity Developer to join our new team creating a best-in-class game with an open economy. The successful candidate will be able to bring together an excellent technical team, define the solution's architecture, and make a diverse contribution to the development of a unique NFT game.

  • You will have the following tasks:
  • designing and developing the application architecture;
  • taking the lead in the development of a gaming application;
  • building and developing the technical team;
  • working systematically with technical debt;
  • making reasonable technology choices.
  • What we expect from you:
  • 5+ years of experience in a relevant position;
  • experience in building an effective technical team;
  • excellent knowledge of C# and extensive experience in developing applications on Unity;
  • understanding the principles of game design and optimization;
  • experience in systematic work with technical debt;
  • ability to correctly estimate deadlines for significant tasks and achieve their timely completion;
  • ability to justify your point of view to other experienced developers and negotiate with colleagues from other departments.
  • We offer:
  • exciting and ambitious tasks;
  • contribution to the games enjoyed by millions of players around the world;
  • enhancing skills through a personal development plan and knowledge sharing;
  • culture of openness, transparency, and common sense;
  • bright and memorable events and valuable benefits (like VHI, English courses, consultations with a psychologist and birthday gifts).

We’re looking forward to your application!
